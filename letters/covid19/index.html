April 1, 2020 | Sepcial brainlife.io announcement

To our brainlife.io community,

We hope that you are staying healthy during these difficult times. Our team of students and researchers here at brainlife.io have certainly had their work affected by city and university lockdowns.

We wanted to check in with our brainlife.io community and ensure our computing resources and mainstream analyses pipelines are allowing you to continue your research, despite the lockdowns.

If you run into any trouble during this time, please know you can always reach us in the #general channel of the brainlife.io Slack workspace. Hopefully, that channel can also give you a sense of community during a time of isolation.

Please let our team know if you want to perform analyses that are not currently available on Contact us to submit any announcements!

Don't forget, if you run into any trouble on brainlife.io or want to ask a question about the platform, message Soichi (@hayashis) or Stephanie (@Steph) on Slack.
