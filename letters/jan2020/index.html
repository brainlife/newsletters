New App

Segment tractogram in to fiber categories

Do you have tractography that you want to do a quick quality check on? Maybe you want to see if there are biologically implausible streamlines in your whole brain tractogram or if there‚Äôs a segmentation failing to find a tract of interest. This app categorizes each streamline in your tractography model according to the regions it connects (i.e. fronto-parietal), giving you a window into your data. A quick look at the relative proportions of the output categories can help you determine if your tractography has sufficient quality and if your tract of interest is likely to be found.

See the App

fMRIPrep

fMRIPrep is a collection of state-of-the-art functional MRI processing pipelines developed by Stanford's Poldrack Lab. This toolbox will preprocess functional data through routines for brain extraction and motion estimation, while correcting for geometric distortions and slice timing and aligning to a number of standard output spaces. We've incorporated this powerful processing stream into brainlife.io so you can obtain preprocessed fMRI data. After running this app, you'll have access to cleaned-up functional data for use in your downstream statistical analyses.

For those interested in collecting functional connectivity matrices, check out the companion app: fMRI to Connectivity Matrices.
